JCode Fracture

Generative · AI · Portrait Series

Fracture

A generative art project that integrates artificial intelligence — the first series to deeply embed AI into the creative workflow. An exploration into what it means to exist within the digital world.

Each piece depicts a fragmented portrait, mirroring the way we fragment our own identities across digital platforms. These abstractions open a dialogue about the tension between genuine identity and the carefully constructed personas we share with the world.

Layer 01

Composition

Distorted grid, Voronoi polygons and Delaunay triangulations establish the structural backbone.

Layer 02

Colour & Stroke

Algorithmic brush strokes and colour applied across the fragmented composition, mimicking organic texture.

Layer 03

AI Rendering

A trained model refines and synthesises — structured generative logic meets expressive painterly abstraction.
